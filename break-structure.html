Break Duration Legend
  • 15*1: One 15-minute break
  • 30*1: One 30-minute break
  • 15*2: Two 15-minute breaks
Break Pattern Guide
  • Pattern: Hours after start when breaks occur
  • Example: 2:15, 4:0, 5:15 means breaks at 2h15m, 4h, and 5h15m

Important: Break timings are calculated from your actual login time.

Overtime / Lost Hours Rules (Delhi)

  • In a day with shift only 1.5 hours of overtime or lost hour recovery is allowed.
  • Maximum 10.5 hours overtime allowed in a week.
  • CCP cannot work continuously for 7 days without an off (Overtime or LHR cannot be coded if that makes CCP working for consecutive 7 days).
  • Lost hour window in India is from 17:30 - 09:30 (+1 day) IST.
